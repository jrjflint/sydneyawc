About Us

The Sydney Amateur Winemakers Club is a welcoming community of enthusiasts who gather regularly to share knowledge, exchange ideas, and celebrate the craft of home winemaking. We bring together passionate hobbyist winemakers from across Sydney, united by a love of discovery and the pursuit of excellence in the glass.

At the heart of our club is a commitment to education, community, and appreciation for the art and science of winemaking. Through tastings, competitions, and workshops, we not only refine our skills but also deepen our connection to each other and to the long tradition of winemaking.
